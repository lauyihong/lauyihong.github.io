Hi! I'm Yifeng, a grad student at DUSP@MIT , where I merge my curiosity for people's stories with a rigorous study of urban informatics and data-driven optimization. My journey is a blend of different cultures and an exploration into the power of visualization and operation to understand and improve the cities we live in.

As a researcher, I dive into the realms of infrastructure management and spatiotemporal analysis to decode complex urban behaviors. My work aims to harness these insights to support robust and equitable public policy frameworks. And as a designer, I'm fueled by a desire to unravel human mobility patterns, enhancing civic participation and enriching urban life. My projct expertise includes crafting clear data narratives, developing applications, and contributing to architectural design with a focus on functionality and social impact.
